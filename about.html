About StartupMachine

We're passionate about creating tools that simplify complex tasks and enhance digital productivity. Our mission is to make technology work better for everyone.

Our Story

StartupMachine was founded in 2020 by a team of developers and designers who were frustrated with the limitations of existing productivity tools. We set out to create elegant, efficient solutions that would help people work smarter, not harder.

What began as a small collection of Chrome extensions has grown into a comprehensive suite of digital tools designed to streamline workflows and boost productivity across various platforms.

Today, our tools are used by professionals, students, and creatives around the world who share our passion for efficiency and elegant design.

Our Values

Innovation

We're constantly pushing boundaries to create cutting-edge solutions that solve real problems in new and better ways.

Simplicity

We believe in making complex things beautifully simple. Our tools are powerful yet intuitive, requiring minimal learning curve.

Reliability

We're committed to building tools you can depend on. Quality, security, and performance are at the core of everything we create.

User-Centered

We design with users in mind, constantly gathering feedback and iterating to ensure our tools truly meet your needs.

Our Team

We're a diverse team of developers, designers, and product specialists united by our passion for creating exceptional digital tools. Based across multiple time zones, we bring global perspectives to solving everyday challenges.

Our team combines deep technical expertise with a keen eye for design and user experience. We're constantly learning, experimenting, and refining our craft to deliver the best possible products.
