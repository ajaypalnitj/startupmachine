How It Works

1

Install the Extension

Add Citation Helper to Chrome with a single click from the Chrome Web Store.

2

Browse to Your Source

Navigate to the webpage, journal article, or other online source you want to cite.

3

Click the Extension Icon

Click on the Citation Helper icon in your Chrome toolbar to open the citation panel.

4

Choose Your Format

Select your preferred citation format (APA, MLA, Chicago, etc.).

5

Copy Your Citation

Your perfectly formatted citation is generated automatically. Copy it to your clipboard with one click.
