Privacy Policy for Kindness Reminder Chrome Extension

Last Updated: February 26, 2024

Introduction

Thank you for using the Kindness Reminder Chrome extension. This Privacy Policy explains what information the extension collects, how it uses that information, and how your data is protected.

Information Collection and Use

The Kindness Reminder extension is designed with privacy in mind:

  • No Personal Data Collection: The extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from users.
  • Local Storage Only: Any data saved (such as your favorite quotes and settings) is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.
  • No Analytics or Tracking: The extension does not use any analytics, tracking tools, or cookies.
  • No External Services: The extension operates entirely offline and does not communicate with external servers.

Permissions Required

The extension requests the following permissions for the sole purpose of providing its core functionality:

  • Storage: To save your settings and favorite quotes locally on your device.
  • Alarms: To schedule kindness reminders at your preferred frequency.
  • Notifications: To display kindness quotes and reminders as notifications.
  • Tabs: To detect when you're visiting social media sites for contextual reminders.
  • Context Menus: To provide additional functionality through right-click menu options.
  • Clipboard Write: To enable copying quotes to your clipboard when using the share feature.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data is maintained by your browser's built-in security features. We do not have access to this data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the extension's description on the Chrome Web Store and/or in the extension itself.
