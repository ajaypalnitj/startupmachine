About Text Case Converter

This tool allows you to quickly convert your text between different case formats:

  • lowercase: all characters in lowercase
  • UPPERCASE: all characters in uppercase
  • Title Case: first letter of each word capitalized
  • Sentence case: first letter of each sentence capitalized
  • camelCase: no spaces, first word lowercase, subsequent words capitalized
  • PascalCase: no spaces, all words capitalized
  • snake_case: lowercase words separated by underscores
  • kebab-case: lowercase words separated by hyphens
  • CONSTANT_CASE: uppercase words separated by underscores
  • aLtErNaTiNg CaSe: alternating between lowercase and uppercase
  • InVeRsE cAsE: inverts the case of each character
  • Capitalize Each Word: first letter of each word capitalized, preserving other cases
Your Privacy: All text processing happens locally in your browser. Your text is never sent to any server.
