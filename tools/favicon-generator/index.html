About Favicon Generator

A favicon is a small icon associated with a website or web page. It is displayed in the browser's address bar, tabs, bookmarks, and other areas to help users identify your site visually. Having a well-designed favicon enhances your brand recognition and improves user experience.

Why Favicons Matter

  • Brand Recognition - Favicons help users quickly identify your site among multiple open tabs.
  • Professional Appearance - A custom favicon gives your website a more polished and professional look.
  • Improved User Experience - Favicons make it easier for users to navigate back to your site when they have multiple tabs open.
  • Mobile and Desktop Support - Modern favicons support various devices and platforms, from desktop browsers to mobile home screens.

Favicon Best Practices

Use Simple Designs

Favicons are small, so simple designs with clear shapes work best. Avoid complex details that won't be visible at smaller sizes.

Start with a Square Image

Begin with a square image (at least 260x260 pixels) to ensure proper scaling across all favicon sizes.

Consider Transparent Backgrounds

For modern browsers, transparent backgrounds can help your favicon blend seamlessly with different browser themes.

Test Across Browsers

Different browsers display favicons differently, so test your favicon across multiple browsers to ensure it looks good everywhere.

Supported Formats

Our Favicon Generator creates a comprehensive package of favicon files to ensure compatibility across all platforms:

  • ICO Format - The traditional favicon format supported by all browsers.
  • PNG Format - Modern format that supports transparency, used by many browsers and platforms.
  • Apple Touch Icons - Special icons for iOS devices when users add your site to their home screen.
  • Android Icons - Icons for Android devices when users add your site to their home screen.
  • Microsoft Tiles - Icons for Windows devices and Microsoft Edge browser.
