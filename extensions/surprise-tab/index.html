About Surprise Tab

Surprise Tab brings a delightful twist to your browsing. Each new tab greets you with a random surprise—be it a fun fact, a joke, a meme, a beautiful image, or something else unexpected. Customize your experience by choosing your favorite content types!

Key Features

  • Fresh Content: Every new tab shows you something different—cat facts, astronomy images, jokes, art, and more. Never see the same content twice in a row!
  • Customizable: Choose which content types you want to see. Enable only your favorites or enjoy the full variety of surprises through the easy-to-use preferences popup.
  • Fast Loading: Optimized to load quickly so you don't have to wait. Each new tab opens almost instantly with fresh content ready to explore.
  • Beautiful Design: Clean, modern interface that's easy on the eyes. Enjoy content displayed in a visually appealing way with responsive design for all screen sizes.
