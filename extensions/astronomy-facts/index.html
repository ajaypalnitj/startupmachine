Astronomy Facts

Explore the wonders of the universe with a new astronomy fact every time you open a tab.

About Astronomy Facts

Turn every new tab into a learning opportunity with Astronomy Facts, a Chrome extension that displays fascinating information about space, planets, stars, and the universe. Whether you're a space enthusiast or just curious about our cosmic neighborhood, this extension makes learning about astronomy part of your daily browsing routine.

Key Features

  • Daily Astronomy Facts: Learn something new about space each time you open a tab.
  • Beautiful Space Imagery: Enjoy stunning astronomy photographs as backgrounds.
  • Educational Content: Access verified scientific information from trusted sources.
  • Fact Categories: Explore different areas of astronomy from planets to galaxies.
  • Fact History: Review previously displayed facts in your personal library.

How It Works

When you open a new tab, Astronomy Facts presents you with an interesting fact about our universe, accompanied by a relevant space image. Each fact is carefully researched and verified for accuracy, providing you with reliable educational content.

The extension includes a diverse collection of astronomy facts covering topics like planets, stars, galaxies, space exploration, black holes, and cosmic phenomena. You can customize your experience by selecting specific astronomy categories that interest you the most.
