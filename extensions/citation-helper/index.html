About Citation Helper

Academic writing requires meticulous attention to citations and references. Citation Helper is a powerful Chrome extension designed to simplify this process, helping students, researchers, and academics generate accurate citations effortlessly.

Key Features

  • One-Click Citations: Generate citations for websites, books, journals, and other sources with a single click.
  • Multiple Citation Styles: Support for APA, MLA, Chicago, Harvard, and other popular citation formats.
  • Citation Management: Save and organize your citations in customizable collections.
  • Export Options: Export your bibliography in various formats including .docx, .pdf, and plain text.
  • Smart Detection: Automatically identifies publication details from web pages for accurate citations.

How It Works

Citation Helper uses advanced algorithms to extract relevant information from web pages, books, and journal articles. When you find a source you want to cite, simply click the extension icon and Citation Helper will generate a properly formatted citation.

The extension maintains a library of your citations, allowing you to organize them by project or paper. When you're ready to create your bibliography, you can export all citations in your preferred format with just a few clicks.
