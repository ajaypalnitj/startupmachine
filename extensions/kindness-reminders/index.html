Kindness Reminders

Gentle reminders to be kind in online interactions with inspirational quotes

About Kindness Reminders

In today's fast-paced digital world, it's easy to forget the importance of kindness in our online interactions. The Kindness Reminders extension is designed to help you maintain a positive and empathetic approach to your digital communications.

With thoughtfully curated inspirational quotes and gentle reminders, this extension encourages mindfulness and compassion in all your online activities.

Key Features

  • Inspirational Quotes: Access a collection of thoughtful kindness quotes at any time by clicking the extension icon
  • New Quote Button: Get a fresh kindness quote with a single click whenever you need inspiration
  • Save Favorites: Save your favorite quotes to revisit them later whenever you need a boost
  • Daily Quote Notifications: Start your day with a kindness quote notification to set a positive tone
  • Share Functionality: Easily copy quotes to share in messages and spread kindness to others
  • Customizable Settings: Adjust notification frequency and features to suit your preferences

Sample Kindness Quotes

"In a world where you can be anything, be kind."
"Kindness is a language which the deaf can hear and the blind can see." — Mark Twain
"No act of kindness, no matter how small, is ever wasted." — Aesop
"Be kind, for everyone you meet is fighting a harder battle." — Plato

How It Works

  1. Install the Kindness Reminders extension from the Chrome Web Store
  2. Click on the extension icon in your browser toolbar to see a kindness quote
  3. Use the refresh button to get a new quote whenever you need inspiration
  4. Customize your notification settings in the options page
  5. Save your favorite quotes for easy access later

Privacy Focused

Your privacy matters to us. The Kindness Reminders extension operates entirely within your browser and doesn't collect any personal data. All your saved quotes and preferences are stored locally on your device.
