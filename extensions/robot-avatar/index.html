Robot Avatar Generator

Create unique, personalized robot avatars for your online profiles with just a few clicks

About Robot Avatar Generator

Stand out online with a unique robot avatar that's completely personalized to you. The Robot Avatar Generator is a Chrome extension that creates custom robot avatars based on any text you enter. Whether you need a profile picture for social media, gaming platforms, or forums, our extension provides endless possibilities.

Powered by the Robohash algorithm, each avatar is deterministically generated - meaning the same text always produces the same robot. This allows you to use your username, email, or any other identifier to create a consistent visual identity across multiple platforms.

Key Features

  • Unique Generation: Create robots based on any text input, ensuring a one-of-a-kind avatar
  • Multiple Robot Sets: Choose from different robot styles to suit your personality
  • Background Options: Select from various background styles or use a transparent background
  • One-Click Copy: Easily copy avatar URLs or download images directly
  • Size Customization: Generate avatars in different resolutions for various platforms
  • Avatar History: Keep track of previously generated avatars for quick access
  • Offline Support: View your saved avatars even without an internet connection

How It Works

  1. Install the Robot Avatar Generator extension from the Chrome Web Store
  2. Click on the extension icon in your browser toolbar
  3. Enter any text in the input field (your username, email, or any text)
  4. Select your preferred robot set and background style
  5. Click "Generate" to create your unique robot avatar
  6. Download the image or copy the URL to use it online

Robot Sets

Our extension features several unique robot sets to choose from:

  • Classic Robots: The original robot designs with a mechanical appeal
  • Monster Robots: Friendly monster-inspired robot designs
  • Robot Heads: Close-up designs focusing just on robot heads
  • Kittens: For those who prefer feline companions over robots

Privacy Focused

The Robot Avatar Generator is designed with your privacy in mind. All avatar generation happens locally or through the secure Robohash API. We don't collect personal information or tracking data, and avatar history is stored locally on your device.
