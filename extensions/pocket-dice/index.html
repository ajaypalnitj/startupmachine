Pocket Dice

A collection of simple, fun dice games right in your browser toolbar

About Pocket Dice

Need dice for a game but don't have any on hand? Pocket Dice gives you instant access to virtual dice and a collection of classic dice games right from your browser toolbar. Whether you're looking for a quick distraction or need a random number generator, Pocket Dice has you covered.

Key Features

  • Four Classic Games: Enjoy Highest Roll, Odds or Evens, Sevens Out, and Pig right in your browser
  • Simple Dice Roller: Need to roll a die? Just click and get a random number instantly
  • Clean Interface: Intuitive design that's easy to use and visually appealing
  • Lightweight: Minimal impact on browser performance, loads instantly
  • Game Instructions: Built-in "How to Play" guides for each dice game

Included Games

  • Highest Roll: Simple dice competition - highest number wins
  • Odds or Evens: Bet on whether the total will be odd or even
  • Sevens Out: Roll until you get a seven - push your luck!
  • Pig: A classic dice game of risk and reward

How It Works

  1. Install the Pocket Dice extension from the Chrome Web Store
  2. Click on the extension icon in your browser toolbar
  3. Select a game from the menu or use the simple dice roller
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to play

Privacy Focused

Pocket Dice operates entirely within your browser and doesn't collect any personal data. All game states are stored locally and only for the duration of your session.
