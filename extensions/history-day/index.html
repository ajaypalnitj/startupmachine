This Day in History

Transform your new tab into a fascinating journey through history

About This Day in History

"This Day in History" is a Chrome extension that transforms your new tab page into an educational journey through time. Every time you open a new tab, you'll discover fascinating historical events, births, and deaths that occurred on the current date throughout history.

Learn something new with every tab and expand your knowledge of history in an effortless, engaging way.

Key Features

  • Daily Historical Events: Discover important events that happened on this day throughout history, from ancient times to recent history.
  • Notable Births & Deaths: Learn about famous individuals who were born or died on today's date and their historical significance.
  • Elegant Design: Enjoy a clean, distraction-free interface with history-themed visuals that enhance the educational experience.
  • Dark Mode Support: Choose between light and dark themes to customize your experience and reduce eye strain.
  • Offline Support: Access historical information even when you're offline, thanks to smart caching technology.
  • Refresh Events: See different historical events from the same day with the click of a button, expanding your knowledge.

Privacy

This extension respects your privacy. It doesn't collect any personal data and only accesses publicly available historical information.
