Privacy Policy

Fortune Cookie Tab Extension

Last updated: April 2, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Fortune Cookie Tab Chrome extension handles user data. Fortune Cookie Tab is designed to provide you with a fun fortune message every time you open a new tab in your browser.

We take your privacy seriously and have designed the extension to minimize data collection while providing a entertaining service.

Information We Collect

Fortune Cookie Tab collects minimal information required for functionality:

  • Local Storage Data: We store your preferences and previously viewed fortunes locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Anonymous Usage Statistics: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to help improve the extension.

How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used exclusively for:

  • Remembering your preferences
  • Tracking which fortunes have been shown to you to minimize repetition
  • Improving the extension functionality

We do not use your data for advertising purposes, nor do we sell or share your information with third parties.

Data Storage

All user preferences and data are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or store your personal information on external servers.

Browser Permissions

Fortune Cookie Tab requires the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save your preferences and fortune history
  • New Tab: To modify the new tab page with fortune cookie messages

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics that help us improve the extension. This includes:

  • Installation and uninstallation events
  • Feature usage
  • Error reporting

The analytics data contains no personally identifiable information.

Children's Privacy

Fortune Cookie Tab is not directed at persons under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your data. You can:

  • Clear all local storage data by uninstalling the extension
  • Customize your preferences through the extension settings

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
