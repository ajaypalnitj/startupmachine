Privacy Policy

Fortune Cookie Tab Extension

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Fortune Cookie Tab extension ("we", "our", or "extension") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe browsing experience.

Last updated: March 2023

Information Collection

We do not collect any personal information. The Fortune Cookie Tab extension:

  • Does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data
  • Does not use cookies or any other tracking technologies
  • Does not have access to your browsing history
  • Does not require any special permissions beyond replacing the new tab page

How The Extension Works

The Fortune Cookie Tab extension operates entirely locally on your device:

  • All content (fortunes, quotes, and facts) is bundled within the extension
  • No external servers are contacted when you use the extension
  • No data leaves your browser when you use the extension
  • All processing is done locally on your device

Third-Party Services

The extension does not integrate with or utilize any third-party services or APIs. It is entirely self-contained and functions independently within your browser.

Chrome Web Store Data Collection

When you download the extension from the Chrome Web Store, Google may collect certain usage statistics such as download counts, active installations, and uninstallations. This information is collected by Google and not by us. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

Children's Privacy

Our extension is suitable for users of all ages and does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will provide a notice through the extension or on our website. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our extension, please contact us at:
