Privacy Policy

Poem Tab Extension

Last updated: April 2, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Poem Tab extension ("we", "our", or "extension") handles your information when you use our Chrome extension. We are committed to respecting your privacy and protecting your personal information.

Information We Collect

The Poem Tab extension:

  • Does not collect any personal information
  • Does not track your browsing history
  • Does not track how you interact with the extension
  • Does not share any data with third parties

How We Use Information

Since we do not collect personal information, we do not use your data for any purposes other than delivering poetry content to your new tab page.

Data Storage

The extension stores the following information locally on your device using Chrome's storage API:

  • Your saved/favorite poems
  • Your extension settings (theme preference, font size)
  • Cached poem data for offline usage

This information never leaves your device and is only stored to provide functionality within the extension.

Browser Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • storage: To save your favorite poems and settings locally on your device
  • chrome://newtab: To replace the default new tab page with our extension

Third-Party Services

The extension uses the PoetryDB API to fetch poem data. When you use the extension, it makes requests to this API. The API does not receive any personal information about you other than standard server logs (IP address, user agent).

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics for our website only, not for the extension itself. This helps us understand how visitors interact with our website. Analytics data is anonymized and does not include personal identifiers.

Children's Privacy

Our extension is suitable for users of all ages and does not collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

Your Rights and Choices

Since all data is processed locally on your device, you maintain complete control over your information. You can:

  • Clear your saved poems and settings through the extension's interface
  • Use Chrome's built-in tools to clear local storage data
  • Uninstall the extension to remove all associated data

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@startupmachine.co

Website: startupmachine.co
