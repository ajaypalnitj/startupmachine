Poem Tab

Transform your new tab into a poetry sanctuary

About Poem Tab

Poem Tab transforms your new tab into a peaceful reading experience, showcasing beautiful poems from renowned poets throughout history. Each time you open a new tab, you'll discover a different piece of poetry that can inspire, comfort, or simply provide a moment of literary appreciation during your busy day.

With a thoughtfully curated collection spanning across different periods, styles, and themes, Poem Tab offers a refreshing literary pause between your online activities.

Key Features

  • Classic Poetry: Explore works from Shakespeare, Dickinson, Frost, Whitman, and other renowned poets
  • Daily Rotation: Discover a different poem with each new tab you open
  • Beautiful Typography: Enjoy poems presented in elegant, readable typefaces designed for literary appreciation
  • Poet Information: Learn about the authors with brief biographical information accompanying each poem
  • Favorites Collection: Save poems you love to revisit them later
  • Minimalist Design: Clean, distraction-free interface that highlights the poetry
  • Category Filters: Customize your experience by selecting preferred poetry styles, eras, or themes
  • Reading Mode Options: Adjust text size, font style, and background for optimal reading comfort

Types of Poetry

  • Classic sonnets from the Renaissance era
  • Romantic poems celebrating nature and emotion
  • Modernist poetry exploring new forms and expressions
  • Contemporary poems addressing modern themes
  • Haiku and other short-form poetry for quick moments of reflection

How It Works

Poem Tab seamlessly integrates with your browser, replacing the default new tab page with a beautifully formatted poem. The extension's algorithm selects poems from our diverse collection, ensuring you encounter different styles and themes over time. Poems are presented in a clean, distraction-free interface that emphasizes readability and aesthetic appreciation.
