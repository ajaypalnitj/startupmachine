Privacy Policy

New Dog Tab Extension

Last updated: April 2, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the New Dog Tab extension ("we", "our", or "extension") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe browsing experience.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from our users. The New Dog Tab extension does not:

  • Collect your browsing history
  • Track your online activity
  • Store personal information
  • Use cookies or local storage to maintain user profiles
  • Share any information with third parties

How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any personal information from users, we do not use your data for any purposes. The extension simply displays dog images and facts in your new tab for your enjoyment.

Browser Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • Replace the new tab page - To display dog images and facts when you open a new tab
  • Internet access - To fetch dog images and facts from external APIs

These are the minimum permissions required for the extension to function properly.

Data Storage

The extension does not persistently store any data. Dog images and facts are temporarily cached in your browser's memory to improve performance but are not permanently stored.

Analytics

We do not use any analytics services to track usage of the extension itself.

Chrome Web Store Data Collection

When you download the extension from the Chrome Web Store, Google may collect certain usage statistics such as download counts, active installations, and uninstallations. This information is collected by Google and not by us. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

Children's Privacy

Our extension is suitable for users of all ages and does not collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Your Rights and Choices

Since all data is processed locally on your device, you maintain complete control over your information. You can clear temporary cache at any time by:

  • Using Chrome's built-in tools to clear browser cache
  • Uninstalling the extension completely

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the extension.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@startupmachine.co

Website: startupmachine.co
