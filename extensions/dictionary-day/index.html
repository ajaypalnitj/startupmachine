Dictionary Day

Expand your vocabulary one word at a time with a new word and definition each day.

About Dictionary Day

Enhance your vocabulary effortlessly with Dictionary Day, a Chrome extension that introduces you to a new word every day. Whether you're a language enthusiast, a student, or simply someone who loves learning, Dictionary Day makes expanding your vocabulary fun and consistent.

Key Features

  • Daily Word: A carefully selected new word each day with its complete definition.
  • Contextual Examples: See how each word is used in sentences to better understand its application.
  • Pronunciation Guide: Learn how to correctly pronounce each word with audio pronunciations.
  • Word History: Access your previously featured words to review them at any time.
  • Custom New Tab: Your word of the day appears on your new tab page for passive learning.

How It Works

Dictionary Day presents a new word in your browser each day. When you open a new tab, you'll see the word, its definition, pronunciation, and example sentences. The extension maintains a history of previous words, allowing you to review them whenever you want.

Over time, this simple daily habit helps you build a stronger vocabulary naturally. You can customize settings like notification preferences and theme options to make the experience your own.
