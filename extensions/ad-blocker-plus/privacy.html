Ad Blocker Plus Privacy Policy

At StartupMachine, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how our Ad Blocker Plus Chrome extension collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

The Ad Blocker Plus extension collects the following information:

  • Basic usage statistics (number of ads blocked, trackers blocked)
  • Custom filter lists you've added (if any)
  • Websites you've whitelisted (if any)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide the core ad and tracker blocking functionality
  • To maintain your custom settings and preferences
  • To improve the extension based on usage patterns
  • To generate anonymous, aggregated statistics about extension effectiveness

Data Storage

All your settings, whitelist, and custom filter lists are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We do not transmit your browsing history or website whitelist to our servers.

Third-Party Services

The extension may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics (for anonymous usage statistics)
  • EasyList and other public filter lists (for ad and tracker blocking)

Each third-party service used by our extension has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your personal data
  • Object to the processing of your personal data

To exercise these rights, you can use the built-in data management tools in the extension settings or uninstall the extension at any time, which will remove all locally stored data.

Security

We are committed to ensuring the security of your information. The Ad Blocker Plus extension:

  • Does not collect your browsing history
  • Does not sell your data to third parties
  • Does not allow any form of tracking by default
  • Uses secure connections for any necessary communications

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the extension.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@startupmachine.co.
