Privacy Policy

Random Cat Facts Extension

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Random Cat Facts extension ("we", "our", or "extension") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe browsing experience.

Last updated: May 28, 2025

Information Collection

We do not collect any personal information from our users. The Random Cat Facts extension does not:

  • Collect your browsing history
  • Track your online activity
  • Store personal information
  • Use cookies or local storage to maintain user profiles
  • Share any information with third parties

Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • Replace the new tab page - To display cat images and facts when you open a new tab
  • Internet access - To fetch cat images and facts from external APIs

These are the minimum permissions required for the extension to function properly.

Data Storage

The extension does not persistently store any data. Cat images and facts are temporarily cached in your browser's memory to improve performance but are not permanently stored.

Chrome Web Store Data Collection

When you download the extension from the Chrome Web Store, Google may collect certain usage statistics such as download counts, active installations, and uninstallations. This information is collected by Google and not by us. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

Children's Privacy

Our extension is suitable for users of all ages and does not collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the extension.

Contact Us
