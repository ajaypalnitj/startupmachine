Privacy Policy

Digital Pet Extension

Introduction

Welcome to the Digital Pet Extension privacy policy. This document outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our browser extension.

Information We Collect

Our extension collects and stores the following information locally in your browser:

  • Pet state (hunger, energy, cleanliness levels)
  • Pet customization settings (selected accessories, appearance)
  • Sound preferences (mute/unmute settings)
  • Premium status and purchased items

All data is stored locally using Chrome's storage API and is not transmitted to any external servers.

Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No personal information is collected
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used

Premium Features

For premium features:

  • Payment processing is handled securely through the Chrome Web Store
  • No payment information is stored by the extension
  • Premium status is stored locally

Children's Privacy

Our extension does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data
  • Delete your stored data
  • Opt-out of premium features
  • Clear all extension data through Chrome's extension management

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at hello@startupmachine.co.
