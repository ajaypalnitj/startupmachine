Digital Pet

Adopt a virtual pet that lives in your browser and keeps you company as you surf the web.

About Digital Pet

The Digital Pet extension brings the joy of pet ownership to your browser. Adopt a cute virtual companion that lives in your Chrome browser, responds to your care, and evolves over time.

Key Features

  • Pet Care Mechanics: Feed, play with, and take care of your pet. Watch its needs change in real-time as you browse the web.
  • Pet Evolution: Your pet grows and evolves based on how you care for it, with multiple evolution paths to discover.
  • Customization: Customize your pet with different accessories, colors, and environments to match your style.
  • Browser Integration: Your pet reacts to your browsing habits and sits comfortably in a corner of your browser.
  • Achievement System: Unlock achievements through your pet care journey and special interactions.

How It Works

Remember the beloved virtual pets of the 90s? We've reimagined this classic concept for the modern web. Your digital pet will sit quietly in the corner of your browser, reacting to your browsing habits and needing occasional care and attention.

Your pet has four main needs: Hunger, Happiness, Energy, and Health. Keep all of these metrics balanced by feeding your pet, playing with it, letting it rest, and maintaining its overall well-being. As you take good care of your pet, it will grow, evolve, and develop special abilities.
