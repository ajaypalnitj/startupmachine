About Mood Tracker

Mood Tracker is a simple yet powerful tool to help you track your emotional wellbeing and develop greater self-awareness. With just a few clicks, you can record your mood, receive personalized recommendations, and gain insights into your emotional patterns over time.

Whether you're looking to improve your emotional intelligence, manage stress, or simply become more mindful of your feelings, Mood Tracker provides the tools you need in an easy-to-use interface.

Key Features

  • Mood Tracking: Record your mood with 5 different emoji options (Excited, Happy, Neutral, Tired, Stressed)
  • Personalized Responses: Receive encouraging messages based on your current mood
  • Smart Recommendations: Get tailored suggestions to enhance or manage your emotional state
  • Inspirational Quotes: View mood-specific quotes from thought leaders and philosophers
  • Mood History: Track your mood over time with an easy-to-use interface
  • Visualizations: See your mood patterns with calendar and distribution charts
  • Achievement System: Unlock achievements as you consistently track your moods
  • Streak Tracking: Monitor your daily mood tracking streak for motivation
  • Customizable Themes: Choose from 6 beautiful themes to personalize your experience
  • Emoji Style Options: Select from 4 different emoji style sets to match your preference
  • Data Management: Export your mood data and control data retention periods

How It Works

Using Mood Tracker is simple:

  1. Click on the Mood Tracker icon in your Chrome toolbar
  2. Select the emoji that best represents your current mood
  3. Read your personalized message, recommendation, and inspirational quote
  4. Track your progress and view insights about your mood patterns over time

The more consistently you track your mood, the more valuable insights you'll gain about your emotional patterns.

Privacy Focused

Your privacy matters to us. All your mood data is stored locally in your browser - we don't collect or transmit any personal information. You're in complete control of your data.
