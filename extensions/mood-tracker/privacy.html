Privacy Policy

Mood Tracker Chrome Extension

Last Updated: May 15, 2024

Introduction

At StartupMachine, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how our Mood Tracker Chrome extension collects, uses, and protects your information. We've designed this extension with privacy in mind, ensuring that your personal data remains under your control.

Data Collection and Storage

The Mood Tracker extension stores all your mood data locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This means:

  • Your mood entries, preferences, and settings are stored only on your device
  • We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your mood data on our servers
  • Your data remains private and accessible only to you

Information We Do Not Collect

We want to be clear about what we do not collect:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, address, etc.)
  • Browsing history or website activity
  • Device information
  • IP addresses
  • Location data
  • Any other personally identifiable information

Extension Permissions

Our extension requires the following permissions to function properly:

  • Storage: To save your mood entries and preferences locally on your device
  • Alarms: To enable optional mood tracking reminders

These permissions are used solely for the functionality of the extension and not for collecting or transmitting your data.

Data Export and Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can export your mood data at any time through the extension's settings
  • You can delete all your stored data from the extension's settings
  • Uninstalling the extension will remove all locally stored data

Analytics

We use anonymous usage analytics to improve the extension. This includes:

  • Installation and uninstallation events
  • Feature usage statistics (without any personal data)
  • Error reports

This information is aggregated and cannot be used to identify individual users.

Third-Party Services

Our extension does not integrate with any third-party services that would have access to your mood data or personal information.

Children's Privacy

Our extension is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
