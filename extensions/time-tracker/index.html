About Time Tracker

Understanding how you spend your time online is the first step toward improving your digital habits. Time Tracker is a powerful Chrome extension that provides detailed insights into your browsing patterns and helps you optimize your productivity.

Key Features

  • Automatic Tracking: Effortlessly monitor time spent on different websites and applications.
  • Detailed Analytics: View comprehensive reports with beautiful visualizations of your online activity.
  • Productivity Categories: Automatically categorize websites as productive, neutral, or distracting.
  • Custom Goals: Set daily and weekly targets for productive time and receive notifications on your progress.
  • Privacy-First: All data is stored locally on your device with optional encrypted cloud backup.

How It Works

Time Tracker runs silently in the background, recording the time you spend on different websites and applications. The extension uses intelligent categorization to classify your activities, helping you understand your productivity patterns.

The intuitive dashboard provides daily, weekly, and monthly reports with detailed breakdowns of your time usage. You can set custom goals and receive gentle reminders when you're spending too much time on distracting sites.
