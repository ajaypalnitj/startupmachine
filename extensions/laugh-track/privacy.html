Privacy Policy

Laugh Track Extension

Introduction

At Laugh Track, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension works and how we handle any data related to your use of the extension.

Last updated: March 2023

What Information We Collect

The short answer: None.

Laugh Track is designed to be a simple, fun extension that doesn't need to collect any personal information. We do not:

  • Track your browsing history
  • Collect personally identifiable information
  • Store any data on our servers
  • Use analytics or tracking tools
  • Share any information with third parties

How The Extension Works

Laugh Track operates entirely within your browser. It detects when you click links, submit forms, or load pages and plays a sitcom laugh track sound effect. Your settings (volume level and on/off state) are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in storage API. These settings never leave your device.

Permissions

Our extension requires the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save your preferences (volume level and on/off state) locally on your device.
  • ActiveTab: To detect interactions like clicking links and submitting forms on the current tab you're using.

We request only the minimum permissions necessary for the extension to function properly.

Third-Party Services

Laugh Track does not use any third-party services, analytics, or APIs. Everything happens locally within your browser.

Chrome Web Store Data Collection

When you download the extension from the Chrome Web Store, Google may collect certain usage statistics such as download counts, active installations, and uninstallations. This information is collected by Google and not by us. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

Children's Privacy

Our extension is suitable for users of all ages and does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this page and, if the changes are significant, we will provide a more prominent notice.
