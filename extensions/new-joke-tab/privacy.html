Privacy Policy

New Joke Tab Extension

Last updated: April 2, 2025

Introduction

At New Joke Tab, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines what information is collected and how it is used when you use our browser extension.

Information We Collect

New Joke Tab does not collect any personal information. The extension only makes requests to the JokeAPI to fetch jokes.

How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any personal information from users, we do not use your data for any purposes. The extension simply displays jokes in your new tab.

Third-Party Services

New Joke Tab uses the JokeAPI to provide jokes. When you use our extension, requests are made to this third-party service to fetch joke content. No personal information is shared with this service.

Content Filtering

Our extension applies content filtering to ensure jokes are appropriate. By default, we filter out jokes that contain nsfw, religious, political, racist, sexist, or explicit content.

Data Storage

New Joke Tab does not store any personal information or browsing history. The extension operates entirely locally on your device and does not maintain any databases of user information.

Browser Permissions

The extension requires only the minimum necessary permissions:

  • New Tab override: To replace the default new tab page with our joke interface
  • Internet Access: To fetch jokes from the JokeAPI

Analytics

We do not use any analytics services to track usage of the extension itself.

Children's Privacy

Our extension is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights and Choices

Since all data is processed locally on your device, you maintain complete control over your information. You can uninstall the extension at any time.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
