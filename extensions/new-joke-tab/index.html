About New Joke Tab

Life's too short not to laugh a little every day. New Joke Tab transforms your browser experience by displaying a fresh, hand-picked joke every time you open a new tab. Start your browsing session with a smile and a momentary break from the seriousness of everyday tasks.

Our library contains thousands of jokes across different categories including puns, one-liners, dad jokes, and witty observationsâ€”all designed to provide a brief moment of amusement without disrupting your workflow.

Key Features

  • Fresh Jokes: Enjoy a different joke each time you open a new tab
  • Multiple Categories: Choose from various joke categories including puns, dad jokes, one-liners, and more
  • Share Functionality: Easily copy and share jokes with friends and colleagues
  • Family-Friendly: Clean humor suitable for all audiences and work environments
  • Customizable Background: Choose from a variety of cheerful background themes
  • Favorites Collection: Save your favorite jokes for later enjoyment
  • Quick Access: Links to your most visited sites remain accessible

Sample Jokes

Why don't scientists trust atoms?

Because they make up everything!

How does a penguin build its house?

Igloos it together!

What did the ocean say to the beach?

Nothing, it just waved!

How It Works

  1. Install the New Joke Tab extension from the Chrome Web Store
  2. Open a new tab to see your first joke
  3. Click the refresh button if you'd like to see a different joke
  4. Use the category filter to select your preferred joke types
  5. Click the heart icon to save jokes to your favorites

Privacy Focused

New Joke Tab respects your privacy. The extension operates entirely within your browser and doesn't collect personal data. Your joke preferences and saved favorites are stored locally on your device.
