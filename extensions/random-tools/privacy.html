Privacy Policy

Random Tools Extension

Last updated: March 8, 2025

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how the Random Tools Chrome extension handles user data (or rather, doesn't handle any data at all). We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of our users.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information or user data. The Random Tools extension:

  • Works completely offline
  • Does not require internet access
  • Does not track your browsing activity
  • Does not store any personal information
  • Does not use cookies
  • Does not connect to any external servers

Local Storage

The extension only uses your browser's local storage to save your sound preference setting (on/off). This data is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

Permissions

Random Tools requires no special permissions to operate. It only needs the standard access necessary for a browser extension to function.

Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will provide a notice when you update the extension.
