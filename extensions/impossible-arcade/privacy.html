Privacy Policy

Impossible Arcade Extension

Last updated: March 7, 2024

Introduction

We at StartupMachine.co respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our "Impossible Arcade" Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

Our extension is designed to be privacy-friendly. We collect:

  • Game Progress and High Scores: This information is stored locally on your device to preserve your game progress and achievements between sessions.
  • Extension Settings: Any personalization settings you configure are stored locally on your device.

We do not collect any personal information, browsing history, or other sensitive data.

How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used exclusively to:

  • Save your game progress and high scores
  • Remember your personalized settings
  • Improve the functionality and user experience of the extension

Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device using standard Chrome storage APIs. Your information does not leave your device and is not shared with us or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

Our extension does not use any third-party services or analytics tools that would collect or process your data.

Children's Privacy

Our extension is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age.

Your Rights and Choices

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you maintain complete control over your information. You can clear all stored data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the extension from Chrome
  • Using Chrome's built-in tools to clear extension data

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the extension accordingly.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@startupmachine.co
