About Nyan Cat Progress Bar

Tired of staring at that same old boring loading bar every time you open a webpage? Say goodbye to dull progress and hello to rainbow-powered page loads with the Nyan Cat Progress Bar!

This fun and lightweight Chrome extension replaces the standard page loading progress indicator with the iconic and endlessly entertaining Nyan Cat animation. Watch as Nyan Cat flies across the top of your screen, leaving a trail of rainbows as your pages load.

Features

  • Replaces the default progress bar: Instantly transforms page loading into a delightful experience.
  • Features the classic Nyan Cat animation: Enjoy the original meme magic every time you browse.
  • Lightweight and fast: Doesn't slow down your browsing experience.
  • Easy to install and use: Just add to Chrome and enjoy!
  • Brings a smile to your face: Because who doesn't love Nyan Cat?

How It Works

The Nyan Cat Progress Bar seamlessly integrates with your Chrome browser, replacing that boring blue loading bar at the top of your screen with the delightful Nyan Cat animation. Every time you navigate to a new page or wait for content to load, you'll be treated to a rainbow-powered experience!
