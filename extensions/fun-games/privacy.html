Privacy Policy

Fun Games Extension

Last updated: August 2023

Introduction

Thank you for using the Fun Games Chrome Extension. This Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to protecting your privacy and explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information.

Information We Collect

The Fun Games Chrome Extension does not collect any personal information from its users. We do not:

  • Collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Track your browsing history or activities
  • Access any of your browser data
  • Use cookies or other tracking technologies
  • Send any data to external servers

Local Storage Usage

The extension uses Chrome's local storage API solely to:

  • Save your game scores and progress
  • Remember your sound preferences (mute/unmute)

This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third parties.

Permissions

The Fun Games Chrome Extension requires minimal permissions to function:

  • It can be accessed via a browser action (the extension icon)
  • It does not require access to your browsing data or history
  • It does not request access to your tabs or windows

Third-Party Services

The Fun Games Chrome Extension does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or libraries that would collect user data.

Children's Privacy

The Fun Games Chrome Extension is suitable for users of all ages and does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and we encourage users to check this policy periodically for updates.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
