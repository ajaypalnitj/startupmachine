Fun Games

A collection of six casual games to play right in your browser

About Fun Games

Fun Games is a Chrome extension that brings six classic mini-games directly to your browser. Perfect for quick breaks, waiting times, or just when you need a moment of entertainment without leaving your browser.

All games run completely offline and require no internet connection once installed. The extension is lightweight, fast, and provides hours of casual entertainment.

Games Included

Rock Paper Scissors

Play the classic game against the computer. Choose between rock, paper, and scissors and see who wins! Rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, and paper beats rock.

Number Guessing Game

Can you guess the secret number? The computer will pick a random number between 1 and 100, and you need to guess it with hints about whether your guess is too high or too low.

Hangman

The classic word guessing game. Guess the word letter by letter before the stick figure is complete. Words come from various categories with helpful hints.

Tic-tac-toe

Play against the computer in this strategic game. You're X, the computer is O. Get three in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally to win!

Word Scramble

Unscramble letters to form words. The game provides a hint for each scrambled word, and you'll get points for every word you successfully unscramble.

Snake

Control a snake to eat food and grow without hitting walls or yourself. The game gets progressively harder as your snake grows longer.

Features

  • Six different casual games in one compact extension
  • Intuitive controls with keyboard support for applicable games
  • Sound effects for all games with a global mute option
  • Score tracking with best score saved
  • Compact design optimized for Chrome's popup interface
  • Mobile-friendly design that works on all screen sizes
  • No internet connection required after installation
  • No ads or in-app purchases - completely free to use

How to Use

  1. Install the extension from the Chrome Web Store
  2. Click on the Fun Games icon in your Chrome toolbar
  3. Select any game from the menu to start playing
  4. Click the "Back" button to return to the games menu at any time
