Color Palette Generator

Create beautiful color palettes for your design projects with a single click

About Color Palette Generator

Generate harmonious color palettes to enhance your web design, graphic design, and UI/UX projects. Our Color Palette Generator Chrome extension makes it easy to create, save, and export beautiful color schemes with just a few clicks.

Key Features

  • Generate Palettes: Create random color palettes or use our smart algorithm to generate harmonious color combinations. Adjust and fine-tune colors to match your project needs.
  • Save & Organize: Save your favorite palettes for later use. Organize and manage your collection of color schemes directly in your browser.
  • Export & Copy: Export your palettes as CSS variables, SCSS, or HEX codes. Copy color codes directly to your clipboard with a single click.
  • Color Harmony: Use color theory principles to create aesthetically pleasing combinations including complementary, analogous, and triadic schemes.
  • Accessibility Check: Verify contrast ratios to ensure your color choices meet accessibility standards for web design.

Who Is This For?

  • Web Developers - Generate CSS color schemes for your websites and applications
  • UI/UX Designers - Create consistent and accessible color palettes for your interfaces
  • Graphic Designers - Find perfect color combinations for your design projects
  • Digital Artists - Discover inspiring color schemes for your digital artwork
  • Content Creators - Maintain visual consistency across your content with saved palettes

Privacy

Your privacy matters to us. The Color Palette Generator extension is designed with privacy in mind:

  • All data (saved palettes) is stored locally on your device
  • No personal information is collected or transmitted
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used
  • Minimal permissions required for functionality
