Focus Mode

Block distracting websites and stay focused on your important tasks with customizable focus sessions.

About Focus Mode

In a world full of digital distractions, maintaining focus can be challenging. Focus Mode is a powerful Chrome extension designed to help you stay on task and maximize your productivity.

Key Features

  • Website Blocking: Create custom lists of distracting websites to block during focus sessions.
  • Pomodoro Timer: Built-in Pomodoro technique timer with customizable work and break intervals.
  • Focus Statistics: Track your productivity with detailed statistics and insights.
  • Scheduled Sessions: Plan your focus sessions in advance to match your daily schedule.
  • Distraction-Free Mode: Hide social media feeds, notifications, and other distracting elements on allowed websites.

How It Works

Focus Mode allows you to create customized focus sessions where distracting websites are blocked. You can set up different profiles for various types of work (coding, writing, studying) with specific blocked sites for each.

The extension integrates the Pomodoro technique, alternating focused work periods with short breaks to maintain optimal productivity. During focus sessions, attempts to access blocked sites will be redirected to a motivational page reminding you of your goals.
